LeaflEat

This is a test website to demonstrate the functionality of LeaflEat.

As restaurants introduced customers back to eat, paper menus disappeared in place of QR codes.

To take advantage of this trend, my idea is to inject an ad into this user journey of scanning the QR code then viewing the menu,

This is a B2B model where if an ad generates $1 in revenue, we (LeaflEat) keep $0.80 and give the restaurant $0.20 or something like that.

My rationale. Yes people hate ads BUT I believe that people are desensitized to seeing them. Moreover, displaying an ad barely interripts the user experience.


You will be automatically redirected to your menu in 5 seconds...
